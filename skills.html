Why is Idaho the best state, you ask? Well, it's quite simple really. First off, where else can you find potatoes that are as famous as their residents? Secondly, who needs the ocean when you've got thousands of miles of pristine, crystal-clear rivers to explore? Finally, where else can you find a town called Chicken Dinner Road? You can't make this stuff up, folks. Idaho, the state where even the street names are delicious.
